About ISA

The Indian Student Association at the University of Oklahoma is a vibrant community dedicated to celebrating and sharing Indian culture with the OU campus. We provide a supportive network for Indian students while promoting cultural awareness and diversity.

Through cultural events, educational initiatives, and social gatherings, we create a bridge between Indian traditions and the broader university community.

Cultural Highlights

Diwali Night

Our annual celebration of the festival of lights with performances, traditional food, and festivities.

Holi Festival

Experience the vibrant celebration of colors and spring with music, dance, and traditional activities.

Cultural Showcase

An immersive showcase of Indian arts, crafts, cuisine, and performances from different regions.
