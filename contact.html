Frequently Asked Questions

How can I join the Indian Student Association?

Joining ISA is easy! We welcome students from all backgrounds. You can sign up at any of our events or contact us directly through this form. There's a small membership fee that helps support our activities throughout the year.

Do I need to be Indian to join ISA?

Not at all! ISA welcomes students from all cultural backgrounds who are interested in learning about and experiencing Indian culture. Our events are open to everyone in the OU community.

How can I volunteer for ISA events?

We're always looking for volunteers to help with our events! You can sign up to volunteer through our contact form or reach out to us directly via email. Volunteering is a great way to get involved, meet new people, and develop leadership skills.

Are ISA events only for OU students?

Most of our events are open to the wider community, though some may be limited to OU students and staff. Check the specific event details or contact us if you're unsure about eligibility for a particular event.
