Upcoming Events

Join us for these exciting upcoming celebrations and gatherings.

Diwali Night 2023

November 12, 2023

OU Student Union, Ballroom

6:00 PM - 10:00 PM

Join us for our biggest event of the year as we celebrate Diwali, the Festival of Lights! The evening will feature traditional performances, authentic Indian cuisine, and plenty of opportunities to learn about and experience Indian culture.

Highlights include classical and Bollywood dance performances, a fashion show featuring traditional Indian attire, a delicious Indian dinner, and an energetic DJ to end the night.

Republic Day Celebration

January 26, 2024

Dale Hall, Room 200

5:00 PM - 8:00 PM

Celebrate India's Republic Day with us as we commemorate the adoption of the Constitution of India. This event will feature a flag hoisting ceremony, patriotic performances, and a discussion about India's democratic journey.

There will also be a special presentation on India's cultural diversity and unity, followed by light refreshments featuring Indian snacks.

RSVP

Holi Festival of Colors

March 15, 2024

South Oval, OU Campus

2:00 PM - 5:00 PM

Experience the joy and vibrancy of Holi, India's famous festival of colors! Join us for an afternoon of fun as we celebrate the arrival of spring with music, dance, and, of course, colors!

We'll provide eco-friendly colors, water guns, traditional sweets, and refreshing beverages. Come dressed in white clothing that you don't mind getting colorful, and prepare for an unforgettable experience!
